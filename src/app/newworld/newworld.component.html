OVERVIEW

My studio, Blind Squirrel Games, has been contracted by Amazon Games Studio to work on their MMORPG, New World. I spent the last year embedded in their UX/UI team, converting wireframes into pixel-perfect mockups, creating UI art assets, and conducting UX reviews on existing in-game features. Here are a few of my projects while working on New World.

ROLE & DURATION

UI/UX Artist

Visual Design, Iconography

May 2022 - present

Leaderboards

Leaderboards is new features that allowed players to compete in various categories and modes in order to claim exclusive titles and/or rewards. I was handed the UX design document and tasked with turning the low/medium fidelity wireframes into pixel-perfect, game-ready mockups while following the New World visual style.


Category Select

This is the landing page where players can see all the categories and subcategories. Each panel will lead the player to its respective leaderboard spreadsheet. I went through multiple passes at the layout of the panels, and we decided the large imagery and icons previewing the subcategories would showcase each leaderboard best.

Board

This screen features all the subcategories in a the left nav menu, the leaderboard in the middle with the player’s own score displayed under, and rewards on the right.

Reward Celebration

Using existing motion sequences, I put together a celebratory animation to showcase Leaderboard rewards to the player.

Other work

Additionally, I created various icons and artwork used for points of interest on the map and for holiday events.
